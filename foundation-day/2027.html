A Public Convening

The Society intends to hold a public mini-conference and panel discussion in 2027, the event originally planned for Foundation Day 2026 and postponed to allow Year Two's scholarly and infrastructure work to reach completion first.

Nothing further is confirmed. This page will be built out as the Society's Year Three priorities firm into a concrete plan. For the reasoning behind the deferral, see the State of the Society, 2026 address.
