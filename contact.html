Contact

The Iroko Historical Society welcomes inquiries about material donations, research access, institutional support, and partnership opportunities.

General Inquiries

For all inquiries, contact:

senior.curator@irokosociety.org

Research Access Requests

Researchers requesting access to archival materials should include a description of the research purpose, relevant credentials or community affiliations, and the specific collections of interest. See the Access & Use Policy for details.

Material Donations

The Society is actively building capacity to accept donations of archival materials, including personal libraries, manuscript notes, photographs, audio recordings, and digital media. If you hold materials relevant to Africana religious practice and cultural heritage, we welcome the opportunity to discuss potential contributions.

Institutional Support

The Society welcomes inquiries from foundations, academic institutions, and cultural organizations regarding grants, partnerships, and collaborative projects. Contact us to discuss how your organization can support the preservation and ethical stewardship of Afro-Atlantic cultural heritage.
