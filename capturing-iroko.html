The Archive · Visual Holdings

Visual Ethnography

Délé Fágbèmí Ɔ̀. maintains an ongoing visual ethnography portfolio documenting sacred spaces, ritual practice, and community life across the Afro-Atlantic world. These images serve as both primary source documentation and a form of engaged scholarship, recording material culture, built environments, and ceremonial contexts that rarely enter formal archival collections.

All photographs © Délé Fágbèmí Ɔ̀., on loan to Iroko Historical Society. Reproduction or use without written authorization is prohibited.
