Visual Ethnography

Délé Fágbèmí O. maintains an ongoing visual ethnography portfolio documenting sacred spaces, ritual practice, and community life across the Afro-Atlantic world. These images serve as both primary source documentation and a form of engaged scholarship, recording material culture, built environments, and ceremonial contexts that rarely enter formal archival collections.
